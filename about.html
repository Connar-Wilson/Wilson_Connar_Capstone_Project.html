About Then & Now Motors

Then & Now Motors is dedicated to preserving the history, craftsmanship, and character of classic American automobiles. Our mission is to take vehicles that have been worn down by time and restore them into fully functional, visually stunning machines that honor their original design.

Whether it’s a muscle car, a vintage truck, or a timeless family cruiser, we believe every classic vehicle has a story worth saving. Our restoration process focuses on authenticity, attention to detail, and a deep respect for automotive heritage.

Our Restoration Philosophy

We approach every project with the goal of bringing out the best in each vehicle. This means using quality parts, preserving original features when possible, and ensuring the final result reflects both the car’s history and the owner’s vision. Restoration is more than repair — it’s craftsmanship, passion, and respect for the past.
